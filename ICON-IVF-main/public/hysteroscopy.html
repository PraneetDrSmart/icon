Hysteroscopy is used to examine the uterine lining. A lens, light source, and camera are affixed to one end of the hysteroscope. This permits a comprehensive and meticulous examination of the uterine cavity.

At ICON IVF, the best fertility clinic in Nandyal, we have highly accomplished fertility specialists who have performed this procedure on a number of patients successfully.

Hysteroscopy may be used to investigate abnormal uterine bleeding, including:


  • Heaviness of menstrual flow
  • Bleeding between periods
  • Bleeding after menopause

Hysteroscopy can aid in identifying the source of the haemorrhage, such as polyps or fibroids. It can be used to conduct a uterine biopsy in postmenopausal women in order to check for cancerous growth. In certain instances, the procedure may be utilised to treat the underlying issue.

In office hysteroscopy, our best fertility doctors at ICON IVF in Nandyal are renowned for their exceptional skills and expertise.

The duration of an office hysteroscopy ranges from five minutes to thirty minutes, depending on the patient's condition.

In-office hysteroscopy will be performed on a standard examination table. The hysteroscope is inserted gently within the vagina, and sterile water flows through it.

Normal vaginal discharge may include a small amount of water. The water is utilised to separate the uterine walls, which normally contact.

Under direct observation, the hysteroscope is then delicately inserted through the cervix and into the uterus.Once inside the uterus, the experts will conduct a thorough examination for potential abnormalities.
