Semen freezing, also known as sperm cryopreservation, is a process that involves freezing and storing semen for future use. It is a common technique used to preserve male fertility by maintaining viable sperm samples that can be used later for assisted reproductive technology.

If you are unable to ovulate or have irregular ovulation, this treatment can aid you or your partner in developing a mature egg.

Semen freezing is done for a variety of reasons, including medical treatments that could affect sperm quality and quantity, such as chemotherapy, radiation therapy, or surgery. It is also an option for men who are planning to undergo a vasectomy or other surgical procedures that could result in infertility.

Additionally, it can be done by healthy men who want to preserve their fertility for the future.

At ICON IVF, the best fertility centre in Nandyal the semen freezing process involves collecting a semen sample, which is then analyzed for its quality and quantity.

The sample is then mixed with a special solution that protects the sperm during freezing. The solution is then frozen slowly to -196 degrees Celsius using liquid nitrogen and stored in a cryogenic tank for future use.

Semen freezing offers several benefits, including providing a backup of viable sperm in case of fertility problems due to medical treatments or other procedures.

It can also be used to preserve fertility in healthy men who want to start a family later in life, and to store sperm for donation or research purposes. Furthermore, it provides peace of mind to men who may be concerned about their fertility due to genetic or environmental factors.
